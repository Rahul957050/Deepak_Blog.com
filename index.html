Happy Holi to Everyone!

Posted by Deepak kumar on march 12, 2025

Holi holds deep cultural and spiritual significance. The festival commemorates the victory of the righteous Prahlad, a devout follower of Lord Vishnu, over the demoness Holika, who had tried to burn him alive. The story symbolizes the triumph of good over evil and the unwavering faith of a devotee. Holi also signifies the end of winter and the beginning of spring, symbolizing renewal, rebirth, and new beginnings.
